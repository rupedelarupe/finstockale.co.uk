Why the ale ?

The Finstock Ale is a fundraiser for Finstock Village Hall. Opened in April 2016, Finstock Village Hall is the centre of community activity, with the design creating a comfortable, modern facility whilst making the most of the views over the Evenlode valley and the Cotswold AONB.
